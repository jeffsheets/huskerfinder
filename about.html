Go Big Red!

Not sure what station to tune to when driving to that wedding? (who schedules a wedding on a gameday anyway!?) Not sure what town you're even close to?

This app was built for you -- Find the radio stations that broadcast Husker football and volleyball games near your location

How It Works

Uses your device's geolocation, then searches through a list of stations that carry Husker sports. Results are sorted by distance from your location to the actual radio tower, with signal strength indicators to help you find the best reception.

Accuracy Note: We use actual FCC tower locations and calculate distances to those towers. Tips for Best Results

Since radio coverage can vary greatly based on terrain, weather, tower power, and other factors:

  • Try the first few stations in your list - don't stop at just the closest one
  • Signal strength bars (‚ñ∞‚ñ∞‚ñ∞‚ñ±) are estimates - always try the nearest towers first
  • AM stations often have better long-distance coverage, especially at night
  • FM stations typically provide clearer audio when in range
  • If you're having trouble with reception, try multiple stations from the list

Alternative Options

If you can't find a clear radio signal, you can always stream the games live at huskers.com/listen. This is especially helpful when traveling outside of Nebraska or in areas with limited radio coverage.

Technical Details

This application uses:

  • Browser geolocation API to determine your position
  • FCC (Federal Communications Commission) tower data for accurate location coordinates
  • Transmitter power data to estimate signal strength
  • Distance calculations using actual tower positions
  • Interactive mapping powered by Leaflet and OpenStreetMap
  • Signal strength estimates using RF propagation models (for reference only)
