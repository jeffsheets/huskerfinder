About This Tool

The Nebraska Husker Radio Finder helps you locate radio stations that broadcast Husker football and volleyball games near your location. Whether you're traveling across Nebraska or beyond, this tool makes it easy to stay connected to your favorite teams!

How It Works

This tool uses your device's geolocation to find your current position, then searches through a database of radio stations that carry Husker sports programming. The stations are sorted by distance from your location, with the nearest ones appearing first.

Important Note: This tool calculates distances based on city locations, not actual radio tower positions or signal coverage areas. It assumes that stations in closer cities will generally provide better reception, but this isn't always accurate.

Tips for Best Results

Since radio coverage can vary greatly based on terrain, weather, tower power, and other factors:

  • Try the first few stations in your list - don't stop at just the closest one
  • AM stations often have better long-distance coverage, especially at night
  • FM stations typically provide clearer audio when in range
  • If you're having trouble with reception, try stations from nearby cities

Alternative Options

If you can't find a clear radio signal, you can always stream the games live at huskers.com/listen. This is especially helpful when traveling outside of Nebraska or in areas with limited radio coverage.
