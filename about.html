How It Works

Uses your device's geolocation, then searches through a list of stations that carry Husker sports. Results are sorted by distance from your location to the actual radio tower, with signal strength indicators to help you find the best reception.

Accuracy Note: We use actual FCC tower locations and calculate distances to those towers. Tips for Best Results

Since radio coverage can vary greatly based on terrain, weather, tower power, and other factors:

  • Try the first few stations in your list - don't stop at just the closest one
  • Signal strength bars (‚ñ∞‚ñ∞‚ñ∞‚ñ±) are estimates - always try the nearest towers first
  • AM stations often have better long-distance coverage, especially at night
  • FM stations typically provide clearer audio when in range
  • If you're having trouble with reception, try multiple stations from the list

Technical Details

This application uses:

  • Browser geolocation API to determine your position
  • FCC (Federal Communications Commission) tower data for accurate location coordinates
  • Transmitter power data to estimate signal strength
  • Distance calculations using actual tower positions
  • Interactive mapping powered by Leaflet and OpenStreetMap
  • Signal strength estimates using RF propagation models (for reference only)

Special thanks to huskers.com for serving as a reference for station information and providing the official streaming option.

Frequently Asked Questions

What radio station plays Nebraska Husker football near me?

Use the Husker Radio Finder to find the nearest station. Click "Find Nearest Stations" to use your GPS location. Major stations include KLIN 1400 AM (Lincoln), KRVN 880 AM (Lexington), and KCRO 660 AM (Omaha). The Huskers Radio Network covers Nebraska, South Dakota, and Kansas with 70+ stations.

What is the flagship radio station for Nebraska Huskers?

The Huskers Radio Network has several flagship stations: KLIN 1400 AM in Lincoln, KRVN 880 AM in Lexington (50,000 watts covering the Great Plains), and KFAB 1110 AM in Omaha. These stations carry all Nebraska football, volleyball, and basketball broadcasts.

Can I listen to Nebraska volleyball and basketball on the radio?

Yes! Many stations broadcast Nebraska volleyball and basketball games. Use the filter checkboxes on the Radio Finder to show only volleyball (VB), men's basketball (MBB), or women's basketball (WBB) stations. The same Huskers Radio Network carries all sports.

How many radio stations broadcast Husker games?

Over 70 unique radio stations (179+ frequencies including AM/FM simulcasts) broadcast Nebraska Huskers games across Nebraska, South Dakota, and Kansas. View the complete station list for all frequencies.

Is my location data private?

Yes! Your location data stays entirely on your device and is never sent to any server. The distance calculations happen in your browser. We don't track or store your location.
